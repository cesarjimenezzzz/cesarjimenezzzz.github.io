Why Cristiano Ronaldo is the best Soccer Player

Cristiano Ronaldo is the most famous athlete on the planet, and he is now a member of Italian powerhouse Juventus after nine seasons with Real Madrid. He is a professional soccer player who has set records while playing for the Manchester United, Real Madrid and Juventus clubs, as well as the Portuguese national team. Cristiano Ronaldo started his career in 1992. Ronaldo has won between 28 and 31 trophies in his career, including six league titles, five European Cups and one European Championship. He has also attained 16 titles from friendly competitions. He also has 54 hat tricks.

Cristiano Ronaldo's Timeline

  • Cristiano Ronaldo born in Madeira, Portugal February 5, 1985
  • He started to play football in a hometown club called Andorinha 1993
  • He moved to CD Nacional, one of the most known teams in Madeira. 1995
  • He transfered to Manchester United for 15 million euros 2003
  • He transfered to Real Madrid for 80 million pounds. 2009
